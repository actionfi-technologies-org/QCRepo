  1. Home Page

    Quick Close is a flexible, device- independent app that allows you to aptly group and promptly track the execution of your project’s multi-step checklists through its life cycle. A checklist is a group of activities (tasks) which when executed, brings an extensive unit of tasks to completion. A user’s homepage shows a centralized view of all the checklists to which the he is linked to. The centralized view allows the user to monitor the progress of execution of tasks in the checklists. Once all the tasks in the checklist is completed, it can be archived for future reference and will disappear from the user’s homepage
